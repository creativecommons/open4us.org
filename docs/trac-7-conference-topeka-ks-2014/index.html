Trac-7 Conference – Topeka KS – 2014

Breaking News!

TRAC-7’s focused, peer-to-peer TAACCCT conference October 1-2, 2014 in Topeka, Kansas at Washburn University is being expanded with OPEN round 1 TAACCCT grantee final conference activities.

The inclusion of OPEN brings a special focus on supporting round 1 TAACCCT grantee participation, showcasing round 1 grantee work, and planning completion and close-out of round 1 projects. Washburn and TRAC-7 have extended capitol city hospitality and a warm welcome to Creative Commons and its OPEN partners. We’re merging our respective TAACCCT grantee conferences into one big collaborative party!
