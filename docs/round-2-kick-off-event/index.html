Round 2 Kick-Off Conference

OPEN Kickoff Conference for DOL TAACCCT Round 2 Grantees

Date: June 20-21, 2013

Location: Minneapolis, MN

Hosts: Creative Commons, Carnegie Mellon Open Learning Initiative, CAST, Washington State Board for Community and Technical Colleges

Invitees: This conference was primarily intended for TAACCCT Round 2 Grantees. However, a selection of TAACCCT Round 1 Grantees will presented their projects and shared experiences developing courses with support of the OPEN Partners.  Select sessions and included graphic recording led by Giulia Forsythe.  Representatives from the US Department of Labor, Department of Education, and the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation were in attendance at this two-day event.

Conference Materials Available

Please see the links below for presentation materials and handouts from the Round 2 Kick-Off event.  Presentation materials are hosted on Slideshare, and images are hosted on Flickr.  All material can be downloaded and reused via the CC BY license.  Links to the webinar recordings will open in your browser via Blackboard Collaborate.
